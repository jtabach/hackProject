Welcome to NHL Career Mode



   NHL Career Mode allows you to simulate your career as an NHL player beginning with the NHL rookie draft through as many seasons as you desire. You will have the ability to select your season and playoff series length.


   To qualify for the playoffs, your team must win at least half their games. The playoff structure is as follows: Conference Quarterfinals, Conference Semifinals, Conference Finals, and Stanley Cup Finals. You must win all 4 playoff series to win the Stanley Cup.


   In order to win more games and improve your stats, it is important that you update your skills regularly. You will begin with a base skill set between 60 and 70 overall and 9 attribute points to use. Each game you will have the opportunity to earn attribute points. If you register at least 1 point (goal or assist), you will earn 1 attribute point. 3 or more points and you will earn 2 attribute points.


   If you reach 85 overall you will have a small but direct impact on your team's likeliness to win games. Reach 99 overall, and that impact becomes much larger.


   Your stats are recorded every season and playoffs and can be viewed by clicking on the Player Stats button. In addition, you will have your My Player where you can keep track of a variety of statistics.


   By clicking on the My Player button, you will see 12 possible achievements and your career stats. Hover your mouse over each achievement to see how to unlock it. It is recommended that if you wish to try and earn all of the achievements, that you play at least a 12 game season.


IMPORTANT: If you refresh or close the page you will lose all of your progress. Good Luck!
